Fireside Chats with Shereef

8/31/14

I liked the video and what Shereef had to offer with his insight. I felt like I was getting vicariously called out from time to time and I appreciated that.

One of my favorite points is his first one of the experiment versus the consumer. Can I come in and expect myself to own it and no one else owes me anything else? Or do I expect everything to be handed to me on a platter? I agree that so far I expect some of the latter since I am paying for it, but I can see that I will find the most value if I have the attitude of the former.

I appreciate that DBC is looking to create good value and not simply find revenue. So far it has been good overall, but sometimes a bit tedious with the writing/reflections.

I honestly have had some thoughts about having this attitude of I create it and wondering if it is really necessary for me to go to DBC. Could I do it on my own? Certainly it is possible. But right now I'm giving DBC a chance because I believe that there is more to it than simply coding knowledge, but that the network and other components offered can assist me in creating the value.
